01 What is an eSIM and how does it work?

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM card built directly into your device. Instead of inserting a physical SIM card, you simply scan a QR code to download your data plan instantly. This allows you to switch between multiple networks easily while traveling.

02 How do I activate my eSIM?

After purchasing your plan, you'll receive a QR code. Open your phone's cellular settings, tap on Add Cellular Plan, scan the QR code, and your eSIM will activate in minutes.

  • Purchase: Buy your plan and receive QR code via email
  • Scan: Go to Settings > Cellular > Add Cellular Plan
  • Activate: Follow on-screen instructions to complete setup
03 Can I use my regular phone number?

Yes, absolutely! eSIM works alongside your physical SIM as a secondary line. Your phone can intelligently switch between both lines. You keep your local number for calls/texts while using mapians for high-speed data abroad.
